Panda2048

Panda2048 is a classic 2048 game powered with magical panda tile.

How to play

* Move - Move tiles by swipe up, down, left or right.
* Merge - Two tiles with same number will merge into one when they meet.
* 2048 - Merge to get tile of 4, 8, 16, 32,... till 2048!
* Panda - 2048 is too far away? Panda will come and help at anytime.
